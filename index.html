This is the real deal! A fully restored 1992 Honda NR750 RC40, which is currently located in the central United States near Minneapolis Minnesota. This bike was the prized possession of the late motorcycle collector and longtime motorcycle journalist Tracy Hagen's personal collection.

In 2005, Tracy brought this motorcycle to Minnesota from Kingdom of Thailand. About 5 1/2 years ago she was completely rebuilt with all geniue Honda parts. There are several spare parts including a gas tank and windscreen that comes with the purchase. By the way, yes we have the mirrors, they were strategically removed to allow for storage in it's custom built container.
